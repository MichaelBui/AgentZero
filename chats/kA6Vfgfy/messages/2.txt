How can CTOs build trust and foster a strong team culture in remote engineering teams?
https://www.revelo.com/blog/remote-team-management-complete-guide-ctos
Building trust and creating a strong sense of team culture in remote engineering teams takes deliberate effort. It starts with establishing clear communication channels and setting expectations so that everyone stays informed and feels included. Regular check-ins and virtual team-building activities can go a long way in fostering collaboration and building connections among team members. Leadership is at the heart of this process. By showing transparency, empathy, and consistency, leaders can build trust within the team. Taking the time to understand each team member’s unique needs and making the most of time zone overlaps - particularly with nearshore teams in Latin America - can strengthen real-time collaboration and personal relationships. When CTOs focus on open communication, meaningful social connections, and supportive leadership, they create an environment where remote teams can thrive together.
