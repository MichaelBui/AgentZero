ROCm+Linux on AMD Strix Halo: January 2026 Stable Configurations

Sharing to spread awareness to Strix Halo users who may find this helpful. TLDR Use linux-firmware-2026010 or newer (avoid linux-firmware-20251125) Use linux-kernel 6.18.4 or newer Use toolboxes with rocm-7-nightly (or rocm-7.2+ when released) | OP maintains https://github.com/kyuz0/amd-strix-halo-gfx1151-toolboxes Pretty much two huge unrelated issues that was the cause of instability for gfx1151 according to OP. Linux firmware bug: Back in Nov, AMD pushed a fw update in linux-firmware-20251125 that broke support, failing to initialize ROCm. AMD reverted the fw, but some distros didn't follow up, so now latest fw has the fix. VGPR mismatch: Underlying stability issues. You'll notice in your workflows, comfyui being one of them, that would cause system hangs and gpu kernel crashes/resets. The issues was a VGPR mismatch (explained in video), between the gpu driver in the linux kernel and ROCm (versions). The mismatch was fixed in kernel 6.18.4, BUT, what's important is the ROCm version, older builds don't match the proper kernel changes, ROCm nightlies from theRock and future ROCm 7.2 work. OP has made a table so you can check compatibility. More on reddit.com
