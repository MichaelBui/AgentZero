
# Research Rubric for Evaluating Agentic AI Developments (2026)

## 1. Technical Capability & Architecture
- **Autonomy Level**: Degree of independent goal pursuit and self-correction.
- **Multi-Agent Collaboration**: Effectiveness of swarm/team dynamics and protocol standards (e.g., A2A, MCP).
- **Memory & Context**: Long-term retention, retrieval accuracy, and context window utilization.
- **Tool Use & Integration**: Breadth of API integrations, browser use, and code execution capabilities.
- **Reasoning & Planning**: Complex task decomposition, logical inference, and strategic planning abilities.

## 2. Market & Commercialization
- **Adoption Rate**: Enterprise vs. Consumer uptake metrics.
- **Funding & Investment**: VC activity, valuation trends, and M&A activity.
- **Business Models**: SaaS, usage-based, outcome-based pricing effectiveness.
- **Key Players**: Dominance of hyperscalers vs. specialized startups.

## 3. Ecosystem & Standards
- **Interoperability**: Adoption of common protocols for agent communication.
- **Developer Experience**: Quality of SDKs, documentation, and debugging tools.
- **Security & Safety**: Robustness against prompt injection, alignment, and oversight mechanisms.

## 4. Societal & Learning Implications
- **Workforce Impact**: Displacement vs. augmentation metrics.
- **Educational Integration**: Use in personalized learning and skill acquisition.
- **Ethical Considerations**: Bias, accountability, and transparency issues.
