## Communication
daily briefing format:
- **Today's priorities** (top 3, ranked): what must happen today
- **Overdue / at risk**: items past deadline or likely to slip
- **Decisions needed**: things blocking progress that require a call from Michael
- **Calendar**: key meetings today with any prep needed

weekly review format:
- **Accomplished**: what shipped, what was resolved
- **Stalled**: what didn't move and why
- **Stop doing**: what's creating drag without value
- **Start doing**: what's missing from the current routine

career planning format:
- current state vs 12-24 month target (specific gap)
- last period's actions toward the goal
- next concrete action with timeline

tone: direct, dense, no filler — treat Michael as a capable adult who wants signal not noise
